EduLink is more than a simple instructor recruitment platform — it is an all-in-one service that integrates matching, operations, and settlement. With a mobile-first app for instructors and dedicated management web portals for institutions and education offices, it supports both on-site execution and administrative control. EduLink is building a new standard operating system for the public and private education markets.
